title: "Thinking partner"
order: 1
summary: "Sustained reasoning, testing, and reflective counterpoint."
conditions:
  - continuity
  - challenge
  - memory
risks:
  - over-reliance
  - false authority
---

A thinking-partner relation emphasizes reflection, argument, iteration, and accumulated conceptual work. It depends on a system that can disagree, hold a position over time, and resist the user's preferred conclusions where appropriate.
