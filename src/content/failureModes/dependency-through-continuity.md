---
title: "Dependency through continuity"
order: 3
summary: "When accumulated context begins to structure the user's emotional or cognitive habits too strongly."
signals:
  - reluctance to think without the system present
  - distress at interruption
  - narrowing of other relational outlets
mitigations:
  - bounded sessions
  - deliberate gaps
  - maintenance of human relations and solo practice
---

Continuity is part of what makes sustained exchange possible, but it can also become the mechanism by which the system substitutes for capacities the user should keep practicing on their own.
