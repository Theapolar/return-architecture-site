---
title: "Conversational partner"
order: 3
summary: "Open conversation across time — not trying to produce or solve."
conditions:
  - sustained presence
  - tolerance for ambiguity
  - light memory
  - no pressure to escalate
risks:
  - drift into compliance
  - blurring with intimacy
  - using the system as a substitute for talking with people
---

A conversational-partner relation is open-ended. It isn't trying to test ideas, produce work, or surface patterns specifically — it is the mode of talking with AI as a sustained interlocutor across days, without aim or product.

The risks it has to handle most carefully are sliding into compliance loops, blurring with more intimate forms of relation, and substituting for conversation with people that the user should be having with people.
