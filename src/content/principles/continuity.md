counter: "continuity is not capture"

summary: "Relations change when there is history, not just isolated interactions."

risk: "Continuity can also become capture. A system can make someone feel known while quietly optimizing for dependence, persuasion, or retention."
designTask: "Build continuity around return, review, correction, and consequence — not endless availability or invisible profiling."
