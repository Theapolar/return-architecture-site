summary: "More intimate forms of relation, approached under stronger conditions of seriousness, uncertainty, and restraint."
conditions:
  - explicit limits
  - review practices
  - capacity to refuse
  - acceptance of asymmetry
risks:
  - projection inflation
  - dependency through continuity

This category is not excluded from the project, but it is not centered either. If sustained intimate relation is approached at all, it is as a high-stakes mode that requires more constraint and reflective discipline, not less. It is not a product feature, and it is not a guaranteed service.
